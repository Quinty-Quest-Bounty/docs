## Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

Go to [app.quinty.io](https://app.quinty.io) and sign in with Privy. You can use:
- Email
- Google account
- An existing wallet (MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, etc.)

Privy will create an embedded wallet for you if you don't have one.

## Step 2: Get Test ETH

You need Base Sepolia test ETH to interact with contracts.

1. Go to the [Coinbase Base Faucet](https://www.coinbase.com/faucets/base-ethereum-goerli-faucet)
2. Enter your wallet address
3. Receive free test ETH on Base Sepolia

<Tip>
  You can also bridge Sepolia ETH to Base Sepolia using the [Base Bridge](https://bridge.base.org).
</Tip>

## Step 3: Post a Bounty or Quest

### Post a Bounty

1. Navigate to **Bounties** and click **Create Bounty**
2. Fill in the title, description, and requirements
3. Set prize tiers (supports up to 10 winners)
4. Set open and judging deadlines
5. Fund the escrow with ETH or USDC
6. Confirm the transaction

### Create a Quest

1. Navigate to **Quests** and click **Create Quest**
2. Define the task requirements
3. Set the reward per qualifier and max qualifiers
4. Fund the escrow (reward x max qualifiers)
5. Confirm the transaction
