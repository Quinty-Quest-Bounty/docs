## The Problem

Traditional bounty and freelance platforms rely on a centralized middleman to hold funds and resolve disputes. This creates several issues:

- **Payment risk** -- Creators can refuse to pay after work is delivered
- **Platform fees** -- Middlemen take 10-20% cuts on every transaction
- **Opaque reputation** -- Ratings are platform-locked and can be manipulated
- **Censorship** -- Platforms can ban users or remove listings arbitrarily

## The Solution

Quinty replaces the middleman with smart contracts on Base.

| Traditional Platform | Quinty |
|---------------------|--------|
| Platform holds funds | Smart contract escrow |
| Platform decides disputes | Automatic slash if creator doesn't judge |
| Reputation locked to platform | Soulbound NFTs owned by the user |
| 10-20% fees | Zero platform fees |
| Trust the company | Trust the code |

## Key Differentiators

**Trustless escrow.** When a creator posts a bounty, funds are locked in the smart contract immediately. The creator cannot withdraw without selecting winners or getting slashed.

**Multi-winner support.** Bounties can have up to 10 ranked prize tiers, distributing rewards across multiple contributors.

**Soulbound reputation.** Achievement badges are minted as non-transferable NFTs. Your reputation belongs to you, not a platform.

**Built on Base.** Base L2 provides low gas fees ($0.01-$0.10 per transaction) and 2-second block times, making on-chain interactions practical for everyday use.
