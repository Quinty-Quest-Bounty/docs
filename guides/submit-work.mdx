## Find a Bounty

Browse open bounties on the **Bounties** page. Each bounty shows:
- Prize tiers and total amount
- Open deadline (when submissions close)
- Number of existing submissions
- The token used (ETH or USDC)

## Review Requirements

Read the bounty description carefully. Understand what the creator expects before starting work.

## Submit Your Work

1. Prepare your deliverable (code, design, document, etc.)
2. Upload your proof to IPFS -- the frontend handles this via Pinata
3. Click **Submit** on the bounty page
4. Pay the 1% deposit (of the total bounty amount)
5. Confirm the transaction

<Note>
  The 1% deposit is refunded to you regardless of whether you win. It exists to prevent spam.
</Note>

## During Judging

After the open deadline passes, the bounty enters the judging phase. The creator reviews all submissions and selects winners.

You cannot modify or withdraw your submission after submitting.

## Getting Paid

If you are selected as a winner:
1. Your prize amount (based on your rank) is credited to your pull balance
2. Your 1% deposit is also refunded to your pull balance
3. Go to your profile or use the withdraw function to claim your funds

See [Withdrawals](/guides/withdrawals) for details on the pull-based withdrawal system.

## If the Creator Doesn't Judge

If the creator misses the judging deadline, anyone can trigger a slash. You will receive:
- Your share of the slash amount (divided equally among all submitters)
- Your full 1% deposit back

Both amounts are credited to your pull balance.
