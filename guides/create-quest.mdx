## What Is a Quest?

A quest is a task where every approved participant receives the same fixed reward. Unlike bounties (which rank winners), quests reward everyone who qualifies.

Use quests for:
- Social media tasks (follow, retweet, post)
- Community participation tasks
- Simple tasks with many participants

## Step 1: Create the Quest

Navigate to **Quests** and click **Create Quest**. Fill in:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Title | Name of the quest |
| Description | What participants need to do |
| Requirements | Specific proof needed for verification |
| Reward per qualifier | Fixed payment for each approved entry |
| Max qualifiers | Maximum number of approved entries |
| Deadline | When the quest ends |
| Token | ETH or USDC |

The total escrow is `reward per qualifier * max qualifiers`.

## Step 2: Fund the Escrow

Confirm the transaction to lock the total reward amount in the contract.

## Step 3: Add Verifiers (Optional)

You can delegate verification to other addresses:
- Go to your quest page
- Click **Add Verifier** and enter the wallet address
- Verifiers can approve or reject entries on your behalf
- A verifier cannot approve their own entry

## Step 4: Review Entries

As entries come in:
1. Review each submission's IPFS proof
2. Approve or reject entries with feedback
3. Approved entries are immediately paid (credited to solver's pull balance)
4. Use batch verification to process multiple entries at once (up to 50)

## Step 5: Finalize

The quest finalizes automatically when max qualifiers is reached. Otherwise:
- You can finalize manually after reviewing all entries
- Anyone can finalize after the deadline passes
- Unused escrow (if fewer qualifiers than max) is refunded to you

## Cancellation

You can cancel a quest and get a full refund, but only if no entries have been approved yet. This works even when the contract is paused.
