---
title: "Quinty"
description: "Trustless bounty and quest platform on Base"
---

Quinty is a decentralized platform where anyone can post bounties, create quests, and build on-chain reputation. All funds are locked in smart contract escrow — no middleman, no trust required.

## Key Features

<Columns cols={3}>
  <Card title="Escrow Bounties" icon="lock">
    Post multi-winner bounties with funds locked in a smart contract. Winners are paid automatically.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Social Quests" icon="clipboard-check">
    Create quests with fixed rewards per qualifier. Delegated verifiers approve entries.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Soulbound Reputation" icon="shield-check">
    Earn non-transferable badges and achievement NFTs based on your on-chain activity.
  </Card>
</Columns>
